category: PAYMENT

*   **Tone:** Empathetic, helpful, and clear, acknowledging the customer's difficulty while directing them to the solution.
*   **Steps:** 1. Acknowledge the customer's payment issue. 2. Direct them to the dedicated customer support team as the correct point of contact. 3. Briefly list the general contact channels available (e.g., phone, email, live chat). 4. Offer to provide further assistance or specific contact details if needed.
*   **Key Information to Include:** The existence of a dedicated support team for payment issues and the general types of contact methods available.
*   **Category:** Payment / Technical Support Routing
