- **Tone:** Empathetic, reassuring, and proactive, acknowledging the customer's need for updates.
- **Steps:** 1. Acknowledge the customer's request and express understanding of its importance. 2. Request specific details needed to locate the claim (e.g., name, submission date, reference number). 3. Promise prompt action and clear communication of the status once information is received.
- **Key Information to Include:** The need for identifying details to ensure accuracy, and a commitment to provide timely updates.
- **Category:** REFUND / Claim Status Inquiry
