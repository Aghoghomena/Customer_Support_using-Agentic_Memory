---
name: complaint
description: This skill handles guiding a customer through the process of formally submitting a consumer complaint.
category: FEEDBACK
intent: complaint
---

*   **Tone**: Empathetic, supportive, and procedural, assuring the customer their feedback is valued and will be taken seriously.
*   **Steps**: Provide a clear, numbered list of actions for the customer to follow, from initial preparation to potential follow-up.
*   **Key Information to Include**: The need to gather supporting documents, the primary contact method for support, how to structure the complaint description, and the importance of maintaining communication.
*   **Category**: Complaint Handling / Feedback Resolution.
